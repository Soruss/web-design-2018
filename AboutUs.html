R.A.Y™ is the world's leading lifestyle brand for gamers. The company has designed and built one of the largest integrated hardware and software platforms for gamers with over 35 million users. R.A.Y™ is transforming interactive entertainment across multiple platforms, including the PC, console and mobile. R.A.Y's award-winning products include an array of hardware and software. Among its signature products are high-performance PC and console peripherals, the R.A.Y line of gaming laptops, software services such as R.A.Y. Autobot (a game optimizer and launcher), and zGold (a virtual currency and payment engine for gamers). The company additionally provides audiovisual certification and IP development services through its subsidiary THX Ltd. Founded in 2005 and headquartered in San Francisco, R.A.Y is backed by Digital Grid, Intel Capital, IDG-Accel and Temasek.
